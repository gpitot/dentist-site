Gary Barutzki

Dental Technician and Dental Prosthetist
Gary has been working in the dental industry for over 30 years.

He first started his dental technician apprenticeship in Melbourne, once completed he continued with a further 4 years study to become a qualified Dental Prosthetist through the Royal Dental Hospital Melbourne.

In 1990 Gary started working for himself in a practise in Doveton, Melbourne. As part of this practise he travelled to different towns in country Victoria. In 2000 Gary moved to Wagga Wagga and opened a practise there, again travelling to several towns around the Riverina.

Gary moved to the Gold Coast Queensland in 2017 and worked at The Gold Coast Denture Centre alongside James Foster, with the outlook of taking over the Burleigh Heads Practise, which he did in January 2018, and changed the name to About Dentures and Mouthguards.

Gary continues his ongoing study requirements as set out by the Australian Health Practitioner Regulation Agency (AHPRA). He attends regular conferences and seminars to assure he stays up to date and is always willing to learn new techniques to improve his service.

Fiona Sutton

Receptionist
Fiona is Gary’s partner and she has worked in banking, retail and Youth work with Juvenile Justice.

Fiona assists Gary with making appointments and creating treatment plans for patients and all other reception duties.

Socially, Gary and Fiona like to travel and spend time with their family, friends and dogs camping and fishing. They are interested in finding new destinations to explore.

Immediate dentures
An immediate denture is a full or partial denture that is designed in collaboration with your dentist, to replace teeth that are yet to be extracted. When your teeth are extracted the denture is ready to be inserted immediately, so you are not left without teeth while the denture is usually being made. Impressions are taken while your teeth are still present.
Implants Retained Over-denture
Implants retained over-dentures are made in conjunction with Dental Specialists to achieve the perfect denture.

An implant-supported denture is a type of overdenture that is supported by and attached to implants. A regular denture rests on the gums, and is not supported by implants. An implant-supported denture is used when a person doesn't have any teeth in the jaw, but has enough bone in the jaw to support implants.
